@InteractLight

Introduction

@InteractLight is an interactive light-installation that makes use of a multitude of input and output channels. It is also a encouraging plattform for experiments. A wide range of sources for input data are available, for example twitter streams, MIDI, web interfaces or a conventional keyboard. Core elements on output side are the custom handmade modular LED-Pixel-Screens, audio signals and a website.

The installation connects the digital social world and translates it to light and color. It allows for visualisation of localised tweets around the world in realtime, in the form of a world map on the LED-Screen â€“ simultaneously the tweets fly past in the projected live stream of all the incoming tweets.

Because @InteractLight itself has a Twitter account, users and spectators can send tweet-commands directly through Twitter. A truly fascinating form of interaction emerges as multiple people start tweeting to @InteracLight.
